PERCY JACKSON



Introduction

A young man embarks on a journey to resolve an ongoing war between the gods after learning that he is a Greek god's descendent. After learning that he is a Greek god's ancestor, the young man embarks on a journey to put a stop to an ongoing conflict between the gods.

Characters:

Percy Jackson

The son of Poseidon and Sally Jackson, Perseus "Percy" Jackson is a Greek demigod who is seventeen years old. He is the Percy Jackson series' primary character and narrator.

Annabeth Chase

Greek demigod Annabeth Chase is the cousin of Norse demigod Magnus Chase and the daughter of the goddess Athena and scholar Frederick Chase. One of the central figures in the Percy Jackson books is also Annabeth.

Grover Underwood

Along with Silenus and Maron, Grover Underwood is a Lord of the Wild and a Satyr. He is a Pan Chosen as well, and he is carrying Pan's soul.

Luke Castellan

The son of Hermes and May Castellan, Luke Castellan was a Greek demigod. He served as one of the Percy Jackson series' primary villains.

Zeus

The Greek deity Zeus is associated with the sky, lightning, thunder, monarchy, honor, and justice. Zeus is said to as the most powerful deity in the entire series. He is the monarch of Olympus, the deity Hera's spouse, and the youngest son of Kronos and Rhea, the Titans.
